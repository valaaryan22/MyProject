Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a flight?

You can book a flight by searching for your desired destination and dates on our homepage and selecting the flight that best suits your needs.

Can I cancel my flight and get a refund?

Yes, you can cancel your flight. The refund depends on the fare conditions of your ticket. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.

How do I check-in for my flight?

Online check-in is available on our website from 24 hours up to 1 hour before the flight departure time.

Can I select my seat when I book a flight?

Yes, you can select your seat during the booking process for most flights.

What is the baggage allowance for my flight?

Baggage allowance varies by ticket type and destination. Please check the specific details for your flight in your ticket information.

How do I know if my flight is on time?

You can check the status of your flight on our website or mobile app. We also send updates via email or SMS if there are any changes to your flight.

What identification do I need for my flight?

You will need a valid passport for international flights. For domestic flights, please check the acceptable forms of ID listed on our website.

Can I book a hotel or car rental with my flight?

Yes, you can easily add a hotel stay or car rental to your booking through our website.

How can I request special assistance?

If you need special assistance, please contact our customer service or indicate your needs during the booking process.

What should I do if I miss my flight?

If you miss your flight, please contact us as soon as possible to discuss your options.
